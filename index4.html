🌏 Arrivals and departures by state in Australia.

The 2 choropleth maps to the right describe arrival and departure changes on a state level.

Notably states such as Western Australia and South Australia have maintained the largest percentage change for arrivals and departures at -97.88% and -93.47% for WA, and -99.19% and -92.83% for SA. These can be attributed to harsh border restrictions in place by state governing teams.

Whereas states such as Queensland and Sydney exhibit lower percentage changes for both arrivals and departures. Queensland from March 2020 to July 2021 had a decrease of 91.96% arrivals and 85.67% departures. Sydney also had a decrease of 81.01% arrivals and 82.00% departures. These could be attributed to less harsh border restrictions in place by state governing teams.

Because of the number of caps to passengers returning and departing, Australian's who are overseas may still find challenges in returning home.
